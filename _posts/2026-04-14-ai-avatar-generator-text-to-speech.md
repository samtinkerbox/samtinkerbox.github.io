## Common Pitfalls I've Encountered (And How to Avoid Them)

### The Uncanny Valley Problem
Some avatars fall into the uncanny valley—realistic enough to expect human behavior, but not quite there. Solution: Choose slightly less realistic avatars that don't trigger this response.

### Voice-Avatar Mismatch
Pairing the wrong voice with an avatar breaks immersion immediately. Match age, personality, and energy levels carefully.

### Over-relying on AI
These are tools, not replacements for good content strategy. Bad scripts produce bad videos, regardless of how advanced your avatar is.

### Ignoring Your Audience
Some audiences aren't ready for AI presenters. Test with focus groups before committing to a full campaign.
